### Job description

Quantitative Trader

**Locations: NJ, PA, MI, and Ontario (Hybrid)**

**About Us:**

We are a rapidly growing trading firm capturing mathematical inefficiencies in alternative markets.

**The Role**

You serve as the central link in a systems-driven execution architecture, deploying capital across your own multi-million dollar portfolio using proprietary +EV models.

- **Execution:** Deploy capital with surgical precision. Follow high-level operational playbooks with zero-error tolerance.
- **Arbitrage and Edge:** Execute high-frequency strategies to capture identified mathematical edges.
- **Technical Integrity:** Monitor account health and system outputs. Identify patterns in data anomalies before they turn into issues.
- **Elite Collaboration:** Work in a flat hierarchy alongside former Ivy League quants, FAANG engineers, and esports pros.

Who You Are

- **High IQ and High Agency:** Problem solver who finds satisfaction in a perfectly executed workflows.
- **Quantitatively Gifted:** Exceptional aptitude with numeracy, probabilistc reasoning, and logical reasoning.
- **Competitive Background:** Competitive achievements or a background in video games, strategy games, poker, trading, or similar.
- **Detail Obsessed:** Zero-leak mindset regarding data integrity and capital deployment.

**Why Join:**

- **Uncapped Comp:** Competitive base plus performance bonuses. It is an \"eat what you kill\" model. Your payout correlates directly to your execution.
- **Direct Access:** No red tape. Propose an improvement directly to the CEO and Head of Quant to ship it.
- **Growth Opportunities:** We're a meritocratic rapidly growing startup with lots of opportunity for individual upwards movement along different paths.
- **Flexibility:** Hybrid remote setup across NJ, PA, MI, and ON.
