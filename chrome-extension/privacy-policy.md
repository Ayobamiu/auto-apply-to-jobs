### **Section: Handshake Extension & Data Collection**

**1. What Data We Collect**
When you use the Handshake Sync Pro Chrome Extension, we collect specific session cookies from your authenticated Handshake portal (e.g., `*.joinhandshake.com`). These include session identifiers necessary to maintain an active connection to your career profile.

**2. How We Use This Data**
The captured session data is used exclusively to:

- Synchronize your Handshake account with the Handshake Sync Pro dashboard.
- Automate the retrieval of job application statuses and career insights on your behalf.
- Provide a "passwordless" integration, ensuring we never ask for or store your Handshake login credentials.

**3. Data Transmission and Storage**

- **Encryption:** All session data is transmitted from the extension to our servers using industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption (HTTPS).
- **Retention:** We store these session identifiers only for as long as they are valid or until you manually disconnect your account. Once a session expires or is revoked, the associated data is deleted from our active database.

**4. Data Sharing and Privacy**
We **do not** sell, rent, or trade your session data or personal information to third parties. This data is used strictly to power the features of the Handshake Sync Pro platform that you have explicitly opted into.

**5. User Control**
You can stop data collection at any time by:

- Logging out of the Handshake Sync Pro extension.
- Uninstalling the extension from your Chrome browser.
- Revoking access via your Handshake Sync Pro account settings.
